The Great Wall Of China

The Great Wall of China is not a wall but a series of fortifications.
The Great Wall of China was built by many Chinese emperors to protect their land and the Silk Road.
And took 2300+ years to construct.




The Great Wall Of China is about 13,171 miles (21,196 kilometers) long.
Here are all the different dynasties:
  • The Qin Dynasty (221–206 BC)
  • The Han Dynasty (206 BC – 220 AD)
  • The Yuan Dynasty (1271–1368)
  • The Ming Dynasty (1368–1644)
  • Post-Ming History (1644–present)
