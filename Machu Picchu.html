Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu is a 15th-century Inca city.
It is located in southern Peru on a 2,430-meter (7,970 ft) mountain valley.
It is located in the Machupicchu District,
Province above the Sacred Valley, which is 80 kilometers (50 mi)
northwest of Cusco. Machu Piccu means "Old Mountain".
Machu Piccu is constatly being eroded by rain and landslides.
People sometimes falsely think that Machu Piccu is
called Huanya Piccu.
When the Spanish and the Inca went to war,
the Spanish couldn't destroy Macchu Piccu because it was so high
up that the Spanish couldn'd see it.
